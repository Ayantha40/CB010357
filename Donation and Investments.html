Investments Unit

The Guide

The Investor Relations Unit (IRU) is the centralized facilitation and promotion centre established within SLTDA to assist potential tourism investors interested in investing in Sri Lanka Tourism. IRU is a one-stop location and point of contact for investors seeking information and assistance on the tourism investments. Specialized staff of IRU are in collaboration all project approving government agencies to help investors to make their investment project a reality.

Recent improvements to the investment approval process have resulted in bringing down the time taken for project approvals up to 40% and is in the process of further improvements with effective facilitation mechanism carried out by a well-trained professional staff.

The IRU is now ready to serve your every need as a Tourism Investor in one of the most sought after holidays destinations in the world, Sri Lanka. We are ready to help you from the moment you consider making an investment till your investment dream is a reality.

OBJECTIVES

Key objectives cited are as follows;
* Present a stress free, convenient approach to investors keen on investing in Sri Lanka’s tourism industry

* Coalesce relevant information for all types of tourism related investments and ensure interested parties have easy access either through meetings, telephone, email or virtual platforms

* Provide and educate interested parties on existing investment opportunities, lands available, projects commenced and tourism need

* Supply feasibility studies and other analyses for potential investments within a short time frame

* Expedite the approval process by centralizing functions of relevant line agencies through a streamlined process within a reduced time frame.

INVESTOR RELATIONS UNIT SERVICES

SERVICES AVAILABLE PRIOR TO INVESTMENT
* Information on potential tourist investments

* Help with investment promotion and gathering information

* Provision of a directory of land available

* Help in finding similar entrepreneurs for investors interested in joint ventures

* Help in completing the application form

* Organization of site inspection

* Representation of the investor at meetings

* Fast-track obtainment of the project clearance permit

SERVICES AVAILABLE DURING THE INVESTMENT

* Fast-track the approval from government agencies

* Coordinating of building plan approvals required for investment

* Coordinating Board of investment incentive schemes granting final approval
SERVICES AVAILABLE DURING THE INVESTMENT
* Recommendation for Visa’s

* Monitor construction progress and facilitation on construction
